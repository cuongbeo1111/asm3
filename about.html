My name is Cuong Nguyen, I am a graphic designer from VietNam and I have experience in Web Design, graphic design. I am crazy about extreme ride. My uncle gave me to try to ride the first time 10 years ago. At that time I was 14 years old and it was breathtaking , and a bit scary. It was in the village near Hanoi. Since my first time, I am crazy about riding, engines, motobike and other supplies. It’s amazing when you’re riding on a high speed at night with bright lights, and fresh air. Also I know how to repair many things in my motobike or how to add some things to my car. I love watching some TV programs about motobikes and all new motobike supplies. In addition I also love off road riding. It is very funny when you are riding down the mountain or across the river. In conclusion I think that my hobby is the best and I wish that anyone will try it.
